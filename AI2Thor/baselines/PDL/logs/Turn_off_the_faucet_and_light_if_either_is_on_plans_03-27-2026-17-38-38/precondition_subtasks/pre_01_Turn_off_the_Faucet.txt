# Initial condition analyze due to previous subtask:
# 1. Robot not at faucet location
# 2. Faucet is on

# SubTask 1: Turn off the Faucet.
    Skills Required: GoToObject, SwitchOff
    Related Objects: faucet(Location=floor)

GoToObject(robot1, faucet)
   Preconditions:
     (not (inaction robot1))
   Effects:
     (at robot1 faucet)

SwitchOff(robot1, faucet)
   Preconditions:
     (not (inaction robot1))
     (at robot1 faucet)
   Effects:
     (switch-off robot1 faucet)
     (not (faucet-on))

Goal condition: 
(switch-off robot1 faucet)
(not (faucet-on))
