PickupObject(robot1, spatula, CounterTop)
   Preconditions:
     (at-location spatula CounterTop)
     (at robot1 spatula)
     (not (inaction robot1))
     (hand-empty robot1)

     (holding robot1 spatula)
     (not (hand-empty robot1))
     (not (at-location spatula CounterTop))
