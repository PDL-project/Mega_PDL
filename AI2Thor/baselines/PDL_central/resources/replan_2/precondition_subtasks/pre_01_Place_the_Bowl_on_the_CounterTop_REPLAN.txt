# SubTask 1: Place the Bowl on the CounterTop.
    Skills Required: GoToObject, PutObject
    Related Objects: bowl(Location=robot1), countertop(Location=kitchen)

GoToObject(robot1, countertop)
   Preconditions:
     (not (inaction robot1))
   Effects:
     (at robot1 countertop)

PutObject(robot1, bowl, countertop)
   Preconditions:
     (holding robot1 bowl)
     (at robot1 countertop)
     (not (inaction robot1))
     (not (is-fridge countertop))
     (not (object-close robot1 countertop))
   Effects:
     (at-location bowl countertop)
     (not (holding robot1 bowl))
     (not (inaction robot1))

Goal condition:
(at-location bowl countertop)
