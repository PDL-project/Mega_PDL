# SubTask 2: Switch off the Light.
    Skills Required: GoToObject, SwitchOff
    Related Objects: LightSwitch(Location=Floor)

GoToObject(robot1, LightSwitch)
   Preconditions:
     (not (inaction robot1))
   Effects:
     (at robot1 LightSwitch)

SwitchOff(robot1, LightSwitch)
   Preconditions:
     (not (inaction robot1))
     (at robot1 LightSwitch)
   Effects:
     (switch-off robot1 LightSwitch)
     (not (switch-on robot1 LightSwitch))

Goal condition:
(switch-off robot1 LightSwitch)
