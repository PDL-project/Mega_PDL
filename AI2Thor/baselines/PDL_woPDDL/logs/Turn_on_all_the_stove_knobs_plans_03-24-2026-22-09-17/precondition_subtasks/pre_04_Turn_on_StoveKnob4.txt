# SubTask 4: Turn on StoveKnob4.
    Skills Required: GoToObject, SwitchOn
    Related Objects: stoveKnob4(Location=floor)

GoToObject(robot1, stoveKnob4)
   Preconditions:
     (not (inaction robot1))
   Effects:
     (at robot1 stoveKnob4)

SwitchOn(robot1, stoveKnob4)
   Preconditions:
     (not (inaction robot1))
     (at robot1 stoveKnob4)
   Effects:
     (switch-on robot1 stoveKnob4)
     (not (switch-off robot1 stoveKnob4))

Goal condition:
(switch-on robot1 stoveKnob4)
